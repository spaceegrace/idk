📅 Your 6-12 Month Healing Checklist

📍 Phase 1: Stabilization & Foundation (Months 1-3)
Build your foundation by prioritizing healing, creating safety, and preparing for sustainable growth.
  • Prioritize PHP (Partial Hospitalization Program) as your primary focus
  • Maintain 1-class-per-term strategy to prevent burnout
  • Secure autism/neurodivergence diagnosis documentation
  • File paperwork with Office of Disability Services for formal accommodations
  • Create a sensory-safe, predictable home environment
0/5
📍 Phase 2: Integration & Skill Maintenance (Months 4-8)
Transition to sustainable routines while gradually rebuilding connection to your interests and goals.
  • Transition from PHP to outpatient care at your own pace
  • Engage with passive technical learning (1-2 hours/week if energy permits)
  • Research sensory-friendly career paths (Accessibility Engineering, Backend Data Architecture)
  • Develop emotional regulation protocols using logical frameworks
  • Check in with therapy team about progress and adjustments needed
0/5
📍 Phase 3: Gradual Re-entry & Long-term Planning (Months 9-12)
Build momentum and envision your future while honoring your unique needs and pace.
  • Evaluate course load—consider 2 classes ONLY if symptoms are stable
  • Build your "quiet" portfolio showcasing technical skills and unique perspective
  • Connect with neurodivergent tech communities (Mentra, SAP Autism at Work, etc.)
  • Plan next steps with your support team
  • Celebrate your resilience and acknowledge your progress
