📅 6-12 Month Strategic Healing Timeline

Phase 1: Stabilization & Foundation (Months 1-3)

Prioritize your Partial Hospitalization Program (PHP) as your primary focus. Maintain your 1-class-per-term strategy and formalize accommodations with disability services. Create a sensory-safe home environment.

  • Trauma processing as primary focus
  • Maintain manageable school load
  • Formalize autism/neurodivergence diagnosis
  • Create predictable home environment

Phase 2: Integration & Skill Maintenance (Months 4-8)

Transition to outpatient care. Engage with low-stakes technical learning if energy permits. Identify sensory-friendly career paths and develop emotional regulation protocols.

  • Move to sustainable therapy rhythm
  • Passive technical learning (1-2 hours/week)
  • Research accessible career paths
  • Build emotional regulation checklists

Phase 3: Gradual Re-entry & Long-term Planning (Months 9-12)

Evaluate course load progression. Build a portfolio showcasing your technical skills and unique perspective. Connect with neurodivergent tech communities.

  • Consider increasing to 2 classes (if stable)
  • Build your "quiet" portfolio
  • Network in neurodivergent tech spaces
  • Celebrate your resilience and progress
